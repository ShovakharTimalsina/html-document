Explore Our Top Study Destinations

Canada

Canada offers globally recognized education and a welcoming environment for students.

Requirements:
  • Minimum GPA of 2.8 for undergraduate studies
  • IELTS score: 6.5 or above
  • Valid Passport
Financial Documents:
  • Proof of tuition fee payment
  • Minimum $10,000 CAD in a GIC account
  • Bank statements (6 months)
Study Options:
  • Scholarships for international students
  • Top courses: Engineering, IT, Healthcare

Australia

Australia is known for world-class education and vibrant cities.

Requirements:
